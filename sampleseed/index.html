The SEED Homes story

Born from a conversation about stability.

SEED Homes began during a charity board meeting focused on helping people find stability. The discussion turned to transitional housing, and a spark ignited.

Six months of exploration, design, and determination followed. The home had to go up quickly. It had to be affordable. And it had to serve the space between tiny homes and conventional builds, where many people have seen the dream of homeownership slip away.

New home warranty through Travelers. Modular Home Builder CSA-A277 is Intertek certified.
